Website Evaluations

https://www.theworldsworstwebsiteever.com/

This website was created to be an example of what exactly could make a website bad for the user and unpleasent to use. So just looking at it shows a number of issues that can be easily seen even at just a glance.


  • Constrast: This website seems to have many points of contrast where it can be hard to read the text due to the poor contrast.
  • Repitition: This website is incredibly inconsistent with its style where almost every section is different.
  • Alignment:The alignment is all over the place for this website where it actively tries to make it harder to read.
  • Proximity: There doesnt appear to be any relation between the content besides the idea that all content is all over the place.


https://www.apple.com/

This website was desinged with the purpose od selling a product. Just looking at the website briefly it is instantly recognizable and clearly conveys its purpose.But it also at a glance appears to have a clean and easily readable design.This makes the website very easy to work with and read and not to harsh on the eyes when it is viewed.

  • Constrast: This website posses very nice contrast that gives it a very clean and modern appearance which works great for this principle. .
  • Repitition: The design of the website is also very good and consistent across the various products that the company sells as far as design .
  • Alignment: The alignment is consistent throughout where key points of products and the product themselves are put in your view and your main focus .
  • Proximity: Related products are grouped around eachother, looking for a new phone? well heres a watch that might go well with that new phone. This is being used to show many related products together .
